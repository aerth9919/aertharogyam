Behind Aerth

A story from the heart of Ayush Sharma, Founder & CEO

“Aerth Arogyam was born from a realization — that our fast-paced, chemically driven lifestyles are pulling us further from our roots. I witnessed people treating symptoms, but rarely the source. This sparked my vision to create a space where ancient Ayurvedic wisdom meets the clarity of modern science.

My mission with Aerth is to guide people not just toward healing, but toward harmony. We combine daily yogic rituals and holistic living with scientific medicine when needed — bringing wellness full circle. It’s not just a brand; it's a movement back to nature, back to balance.”

— Ayush Sharma
Founder & CEO, Aerth Arogyam
